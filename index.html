About Me

Hello! My name is Rich Peaua. I am an experienced Linux Sys Admin/Devops Engineer diving head first into software development. I consider myself a "builder", meaning, I LOVE building things whether it's an awesome shed in the backyard or task-automating python scripts on a server. As much as I like creating and maintaining computer systems, on-prem and in the cloud as a Devops Engineer, my creativity has rarely been pushed to its limits and my unquenchable thirst for building has never even come close to being sated... and that's why this immersion into software developement. I'm convinced it is the remedy to my affliction. From VR to mobile applications, the possibilities for a builder such as I are INFINITE. I have absolutely no experience with full blown software development so this will be quite the journey for me. I will be taking the multi project approach to learning and this website will serve to document and hopefully serve as a form of education and motivation for those also thinking of making the leap into software development.
