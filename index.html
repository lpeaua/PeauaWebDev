About Me

Hello! My name is Rich Peaua. I am an experienced Linux Sys Admin/Devops Engineer diving head first into software development. I consider myself a "builder", meaning, I LOVE building things whether it's an awesome shed in the backyard or task-automating python scripts on a server. I will be taking the multi project approach to learning and this website will serve to document and hopefully educate and motivate those of you who might also thinking of making the leap into software development.
