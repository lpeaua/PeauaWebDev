My Goal: Immersion Into Software Development

February 28th, 2019

The Why:

There aren't many things in this world that provide you the complete and absolute freedom and capability to create things from, relatively nothing, like Software Development does.

There's only so much that can be done with a lump of clay/playdough.

You can only architect buildings within the constraints of the environment and laws of physics.

You can do only do, so much, of anything in this limited physical plane of existance, but what about the Digital plane?

Software development has opened up a world of near infinite opportunities to create! From a dinky little html webpage to a completely immersive, virtual reality rollercoaster ride and.. even beyond - the digital world is, literally, your oyster.

As fun as it is to go off and start creating a whole bunch of amazing, yet random things through software development, the best and most fulfilling thing, to me at least, is being able to apply that new power to create in a way that benefits someone or something. In my case, it's applying it to the benefit of the masses! My dream is to become a part of the development of products and services that will positively effect a change in its users life and oh how excited I am to begin!!

In order to break into the software scene, espececially without formal training or even a college degree, I will be need figure out the best way to gain the knowledge needed and build up some credibility in my newly acquired skills.
